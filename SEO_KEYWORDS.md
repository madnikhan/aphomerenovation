## üîç Keyword Usage Guidelines

### Title Tags (Include Primary Keyword):
- Keep under 60 characters
- Include location when relevant
- Make it compelling

**Examples:**
- "Chimney Removal Services Birmingham | AK Home Renovation"
- "Professional House Renovation UK | Expert Contractors"
- "Plastering & Skimming Services | Birmingham"

### Meta Descriptions (Include Keywords Naturally):
- Keep 150-160 characters
- Include call-to-action
- Include location when relevant

**Examples:**
- "Professional chimney removal services in Birmingham. Safe, compliant removal with full structural assessment. Free quotes. Call today!"
- "Expert house renovation services across the UK. Quality workmanship, competitive pricing. Transform your home today!"

### H1 Tags (Primary Keyword):
- One H1 per page
- Include main keyword
- Make it descriptive

**Examples:**
- "Professional Chimney Removal Services in Birmingham"
- "Complete House Renovation Services Across the UK"

### Content (Natural Keyword Usage):
- Use keywords naturally
- Don't overstuff
- Include variations
- Use related terms
